## 1️⃣ **Kompyuterdan rasm yuklash** (Tavsiya etiladi!) 📁

### Qadamlar:

1. Admin panelni oching: `admin-simple.html`
2. "Yangi mahsulot" formida "Rasm" bo'limigacha scroll qiling
3. **"📁 Kompyuterdan yuklash"** tugmasini bosing
4. Kompyuteringizdan rasm tanlang:
   - ✅ JPG, PNG, WEBP formatlar qo'llab-quvvatlanadi
   - ✅ Maksimal hajm: 5MB
   - ✅ Tavsiya hajm: 500KB gacha (tezroq yuklanadi)

5. Rasm avtomatik preview da ko'rinadi
6. Formani to'ldiring va "Saqlash" tugmasini bosing
7. ✅ Tayyor! Rasm localStorage da saqlanadi

### 💡 Afzalliklari:
- ✅ Internet kerak emas
- ✅ Rasm doim ishlaydi
- ✅ Tezroq yuklash
- ✅ Hosting kerak emas

---

## 2️⃣ **URL orqali rasm qo'shish** 🔗

### Qadamlar:

1. "Rasm" bo'limida **"🔗 URL kiriting"** tugmasini bosing
2. Rasm URL manzilini kiriting (masalan, Unsplash.com dan)
3. **"URL dan yuklash"** tugmasini bosing
4. Rasm preview da ko'rinadi

### 📝 URL topish:

**Unsplash.com:**
1. [Unsplash.com](https://unsplash.com) ga kiring
2. "cosmetics", "perfume" kabi so'zlarni qidiring
3. Rasm ustiga right-click → "Copy image address"
4. URL ni formaga paste qiling

**Masalan:**
```
https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1512496015851-a90fb38ba796?w=500
```

## 📱 Qanday ishlaydi?

### Kompyuterdan yuklash:
1. Rasm tanlanadi
2. JavaScript rasmni **Base64** formatga o'giradi
3. Base64 ma'lumot localStorage ga saqlanadi
4. Mahsulot ko'rsatilganda Base64 dan rasm ko'rsatiladi

### Xotira:
- 1 rasm ≈ 100-500KB (localStorage da)
- localStorage maksimal: 5-10MB (brauzerga bog'liq)
- Taxminan 20-50 ta rasm saqlashingiz mumkin

---

## ⚡ Tezkor maslahatlar:

1. **Rasm hajmini kamaytiring:**
   - [TinyPNG.com](https://tinypng.com) - rasmni siqish
   - Tavsiya o'lcham: 800x800 px
   - Tavsiya hajm: 200-500KB

2. **Rasm formatlar:**
   - ✅ JPG - fotolar uchun
   - ✅ PNG - shaffof fon uchun
   - ✅ WEBP - eng yaxshi siqish

3. **Mahsulot rasmini o'zgartirish:**
   - "Tahrirlash" tugmasini bosing
   - Yangi rasm yuklang yoki URL kiriting
   - "Yangilash" tugmasini bosing
